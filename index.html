Improve your development

In a complex, uncertain, constantly moving world, you need to become agile, able to make and find the right information in the shared multitude. The makers' lab is the key, it allows you to test, collaborate, make, learn and fail. Come manipulate technologies and their usages by yourself!

build

Maker experience focused

Through digital tools, innovative pedagogies, and areas dedicated to co-construction, you will develop new skills and a maker spirit. The limit between atom and bit has never been so fine. The lab gives you access to code and materials that will allow you to rethink the world with your own eyes. In the lab, you are the one making!

group

Learn & share with others

We offer tutorials, examples and events to help you get started! Learn how to quickly prototype connected objects, websites, products, mobile apps, or whatever you're thinking about ! Even better, learn how to do it with others…
