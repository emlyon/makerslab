Do it with others

Makers' labs are unique prototyping laboratories in the landscape of French business schools. Dedicated to creativity and learning by doing, they are accessible to the whole emlyon community: graduates, students, staff, professors, researchers, incubates and corporate partners.

Get a maker experience

Bring your ideas to life in record time thanks to the resources available: 3d printers, laser cutters, CNC milling machines, sewing machines, electronics, etc. Our team of facilitators will guide you to become a maker!

Learn the way you want

We offer three learning styles to discover design, Web, IoT, artificial intelligence and digital manufacturing: online tutorials accessible when you want, experiential events to learn together and blended courses to deepen your knowledge.
