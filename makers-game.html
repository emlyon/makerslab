makers game

Teaching computer science in school is one of the biggest challenge today: how can we encourage people to learn such a complex field as programmation ?
Our answer is “by making coding fun” with a creative retro-gaming workshop !
On computers, game consoles have existed for years. But it is the powerful upswing of the nanocomputers Raspberry Pi that made possible to easily build actual game consoles at a rock-bottom cost.
With our open source arcade table, clear your coffee table from the usual board games and find the timeless games Pong, Snake and Tetris.

Find our code & our CAD files on Github
